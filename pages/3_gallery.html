My happy place

Being in the mountains and becoming one with nature is my go-to therapy to feel happy and refreshed. It all started in my 'home' range, the White Mountains in NH. I've also loved hiking in the National Parks and few other places I've visited. Here are more snapshots from my happy place :)

Winter love

I had never experienced winter or snow the first 20 years of my life, maybe that's why I instantaneously fell in love with winter when I moved to Boston. But I probably wouldn't have gotten into winter hiking without MITOC, their winter school and the community! Some of my favorite clicks in various winter wonderlands while hiking and skiing.

Bird-watching

Check out my amateur shots of some cute birds here. The best thing about birding is that all you need is a pair of binoculars to get started, so most of the photos here are shot on my phone through a 12X binoculars.

Graduation
