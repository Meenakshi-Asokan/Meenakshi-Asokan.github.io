I also contribute to the Caradini/Harris lab github. These repositories are intended for general use and maintained:
  • allenCCF Tools to work with the Allen CCF atlas, I wrote the Neuropixels trajectory explorer 'allen_ccf_npx'. I demo this tool in this video for the 2020 UCL Neuropixels course.
  • AP_histology A simple MATLAB pipeline to analyze histology images (including extracting Neuropixels trajectories). I demo this tool in this video for the 2020 UCL Neuropixels course.
